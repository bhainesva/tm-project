Alipay Owner Said to Plan Shanghai IPO

59:17 - Sources are telling Bloomberg that Alibaba's finance affiliate is planning a public offering in Shanghai. It said that Ant Financial, the owner of Alipay, could start the IPO process as soon as this year and it could fetch China's biggest valuation since 2010. Bloomberg's Lulu Chen reports on "First Up."
