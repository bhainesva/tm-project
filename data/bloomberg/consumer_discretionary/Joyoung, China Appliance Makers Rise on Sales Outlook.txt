Joyoung, China Appliance Makers Rise on Sales Outlook
['Bloomberg News']
http://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2011-02-11/joyoung-china-household-appliance-makers-rise-on-sales-outlook
Joyoung Co. led gains among household appliance makers in Shanghai and Shenzhen trading after Guotai Junan Securities Co. said the industry’s January sales will exceed market predictions.

Joyoung, which makes soybean milk machines for use in homes, jumped 4.5 percent to 16.39 yuan at 3 p.m. close in Shenzhen, the highest close since Dec. 17. Qingdao Haier Co. the air-conditioner and refrigerator unit of China’s biggest appliance maker, added 1.5 percent to 27.81 yuan in Shanghai. A gauge of consumer discretionary stocks rose 1.5 percent, the most among the CSI 300 Index’s 10 industry groups.

Household appliances companies may post earnings growth of more than 30 percent in January, Fang Xin, a Shanghai-based analyst at Guotai Junan Securities, wrote in a report today. Sales increased 21.9 percent during the weeklong Lunar New Year holidays that ended Feb. 8, up from a 15.4 percent gain a year earlier, according to the report.

Gree Electric Appliances Inc. China’s largest maker of home air-conditioners, added 0.2 percent to 18.39 yuan in Shenzhen. Gree’s first-quarter profit may increase more than 40 percent, according to Guotai Junan Securities. GD Midea Holding Co. China’s second-biggest publicly traded appliance maker, gained 0.8 percent to 18.25 yuan in Shenzhen, extending yesterday’s 10 percent advance.
