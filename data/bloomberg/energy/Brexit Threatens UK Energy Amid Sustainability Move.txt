(Bloomberg) -- The UK aims to be coal free by 2023 and this means attracting new, sustainable energy players and investors. Caroline Hyde spoke to First Word Europe’s Sandra Kilhof who interviewed Connie Hedegaard, Anders Eldrup and Angus McNeil at the Danish UK Chamber of Commerce’s UK Energy Business Summit. Former DONG CEO and Special Advisor to investment group Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners, Anders Eldrup, said investors are worried that a Brexit could derail UK climate change targets.
