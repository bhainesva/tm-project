Oil Talks' Failure Risks Denting Asian Stock Rebound- Chart
['Richard Frost']
http://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2016-04-18/oil-talks-failure-risks-denting-asian-stock-rebound-chart
The failure by the world’s biggest oil producers to agree to limit supplies will undercut one of the biggest drivers in Asian equities this year. A gauge of energy producers in MSCI Inc.’s regional benchmark index jumped 21 percent in the past three months to be the best performer among industry groups amid a rebound in oil. The energy measure dropped as much as 1.8 percent on Monday as crude tumbled in New York.
